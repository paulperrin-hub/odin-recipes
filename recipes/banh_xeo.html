Bánh Xèo

Description:

Bánh Xèo is a savory, crispy Vietnamese pancake made with rice flour, turmeric, shrimp, pork, and bean sprouts. It’s typically eaten wrapped in lettuce with herbs and dipped in fish sauce.

Ingredients (make 6 pancakes):

  • 200 g rice flour
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 400 ml coconut milk (or water)
  • 1 spring onion (chopped)
  • 200 g shrimp (peeled)
  • 150 g pork belly (thinly sliced)
  • 200 g bean sprouts
  • Cooking oil

For dipping sauce (Nước Chấm):

  • 3 tbsp fish sauce
  • 3 tbsp lime juice
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1/2 cup warm water
  • 1 garlic clove (minced)
  • 1 chili (sliced)

Steps:

  1. Mix rice flour, cornstarch, turmeric, coconut milk, and spring onion into a thin batter. Rest 30 minutes.
  2. Heat pan with oil, sear pork and shrimp until half-cooked.
  3. Pour in a ladle of batter, swirl to cover pan thinly.
  4. Add bean sprouts on one side, cover, cook until edges crisp.
  5. Fold pancake in half, cook until golden and crispy.
  6. Serve hot with lettuce, herbs, and dipping sauce.
