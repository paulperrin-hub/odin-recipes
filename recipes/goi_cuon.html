Gỏi Cuốn

Description:

Gỏi Cuốn are fresh, light, and healthy rice paper rolls filled with shrimp, pork, vermicelli, and herbs, served with peanut dipping sauce.

Ingredients (makes 8 rolls):

  • 8 rice paper wrappers
  • 100 g vermicelli noodles (cooked)
  • 8 shrimp (boiled, halved lengthwise)
  • 100 g pork belly (boiled, thinly sliced)
  • Fresh herbs: mint, Thai basil, cilantro
  • Lettuce leaves

For peanut dipping sauce:

  • 3 tbsp hoisin sauce
  • 2 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 garlic clove (minced)
  • Crushed peanuts for topping

Steps:

  1. Prepare shrimp and pork, slice thin.
  2. Cook vermicelli noodles, rinse cold.
  3. Dip rice paper in warm water for 3 seconds, lay on a flat surface.
  4. Add lettuce, herbs, noodles, pork, and shrimp (shrimp placed neatly for presentation).
  5. Fold bottom over filling, fold sides, then roll tightly.
  6. For sauce: simmer hoisin, peanut butter, water, sugar, garlic until smooth. Top with peanuts.
  7. Serve fresh with dipping sauce.
