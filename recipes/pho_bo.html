Phở Bò

Description:

Phở is Vietnam's most famous dish - a fragrant noodle soup with tender beef, fresh herbs, and a flavorful broth simmered with spices.

Ingredients (serves 4):

  • 1 kg beef bones (marrow or knuckle)
  • 300 g beef brisket or flank
  • 200 g beef sirloin (thinly sliced)
  • 500 g flat rice noodles (bánh phở)
  • 1 large onion (halved, charred)
  • 1 piece of ginger (8 cm, charred)
  • 3 star anise
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 3 cloves
  • 1 cardamom pod
  • 1 tbsp coriander seeds
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • 2 tbsp rock sugar (or brown sugar)
  • Salt to taste

Garnishes:

  • Bean sprouts
  • Thai basil
  • Cilantro
  • Lime wedges
  • Fresh chilu slices
  • Hoisin sauce & Sriracha

Steps:

  1. Blanch beef bones in boiling water for 5 minutes, then rinse clean.
  2. In a large pot, add bones, brisket, onion, ginger, and 4 liters of water. Simmer 2-3 hours.
  3. Toast spices in a dry pan, then add to broth. Simmer 1 more hour.
  4. Remove brisket, slice thinly. Strain broth, season with fish sauce, sugar, and salt.
  5. Cook noodles per package, divide into bowls.
  6. Add sliced brisket and raw sirloin on top.
  7. Pour hot broth over, serve with garnishes.
